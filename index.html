🌬 Wind Strategy
  • Park cars nose-in — bodies block road-side wind
  • Canopies stake on road-facing edge of Spots 1 & 3
  • Tents positioned in car's wind shadow
  • Back edge of Spot 2: use a tarp as a rear windblock if needed
🍳 Cooking Setup
  • Blackstone stays in Spot 2 only — 10 ft min from any tent
  • Prep table behind grill, coolers flanking both sides
  • Trash bags downwind at back corner of Spot 2
  • Propane stored under prep table, not in car
⚡ Power + Charging
  • Central hub in Spot 2 keeps everyone topped up
  • Label every cable — group of 4–7 = cable chaos
  • Solar panel on car roof or Spot 2 back edge if available
  • Rotate power banks on a schedule each morning
🌙 Sleep Comfort
  • Spot 3 back corner = quietest — assign lightest sleepers to Spots 1/2
  • Tent doors face inward (away from road and grill)
  • Leave 1 ft gap between tents for airflow in the heat
  • Earplugs, eye masks, and ear plugs packed per person
